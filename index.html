THE GAUNTLET

From Morro Rock to Islay Hill. 18 miles of biking, 10 miles of hiking, 4,500 feet of elevation gain.

01

MORRO ROCK to BLACK HILL

The journey begins at the iconic Morro Rock. Mount up and bike to the base of Black Hill, transitioning to your first hike to the summit.

02

CABRILLO

Descend, grab the bike, and push towards Cerro Cabrillo. Leave the wheels behind and tackle the rocky ascent.

03

BISHOP PEAK

The highest of the Morros. Bike to the trailhead and prepare for a grueling climb to the top of SLO's most famous peak.

04

MADONNA (CERRO SAN LUIS)

Back on the saddle. Navigate to the base of Madonna. Your legs are burning, but the hike up the 'M' awaits.

05

ISLAY HILL

The final push. Bike all the way across town to Islay Hill. Empty the tank on the final hike to claim your 5x5 finisher status.

YEAR 3: BIGGER. BADDER.

What started as a crazy idea with 9 people in Year 1 grew to a punishing pack of 16 in Year 2. This year, we're shooting for 50 racers.

Because this race is about community and doing hard things, it remains invite-only. If you are registering, you've earned 3 invitations to bring new blood into the gauntlet. Choose wisely.

THE AFTERPARTY

Survive the peaks, earn the pints. September's suffering leads straight into the Oktober Fest at the Kunz's house. We will also be celebrating Josh and Craig's birthday!

We will be catering dinner from Beda's Biergarten. Folks are encouraged to bring a side and their favorite beverage to share (alcoholic or non-alcoholic).

Families and significant others are highly encouraged to join the celebration! There will be fun activities for the kiddos while the racers swap war stories from the trail.
